# Privacy Policy — Gaussian Splatting Viewer

**Last updated: 2026-07-18**

Gaussian Splatting Viewer ("the App") is developed by Dennis Kroeker. The App renders 3D Gaussian Splatting files (.ply, .splat, .spz) locally on your device using Apple's Metal framework.

## Data We Collect

**None.** The App does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data. There is no analytics, no tracking, no advertising SDKs, and no cloud connectivity. All file processing happens entirely on-device.

## Files You Open

When you open a .ply, .splat, or .spz file, it is loaded from your device's storage (Files app, iCloud Drive, AirDrop, or any Files provider) into the App's memory for rendering. The file content never leaves your device. Files are not uploaded to any server.

## Permissions

- **Files / Document Picker:** Used only when you choose to open a splat file. No file access happens without your explicit action.
- **No network access required:** The App has no network communication. All rendering is local.

## Third-Party Libraries

- [MetalSplatter](https://github.com/scier/MetalSplatter) (MIT License) — Metal-based Gaussian Splatting renderer
- [spz](https://github.com/Niantic/spz) (MIT License) — Compressed Gaussian Splatting format

Neither library transmits any data.

## Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children.
